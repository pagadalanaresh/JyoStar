What’s the difference between “Rent," “Buy,” and Subscription purchases on Vimeo On Demand?

With Vimeo On Demand, you can make three kinds of purchases:

Rent: When you rent videos, you will be able to stream the videos anytime during the specified rental period.

Buy: When you buy videos, you will be able to stream the videos for as long as they remain on Vimeo. If the seller allows, you will also be able to download the videos to your computer and devices, DRM-free.

Subscription: When you subscribe to a series, you have unlimited streaming access to all current episodes of the series for a recurring monthly fee. You will also have immediate access to any new episodes added to the series page during your active subscription.

If I purchase the “Rent All," "Buy All," or Subscription option for a VOD series, will I be able to watch videos that get added after my purchase?

When you "Rent All," you get access to all of the current videos as well as any that are added to the Vimeo on Demand page during the specified rental period.

When you “Buy All,” you get access to any videos added to the VOD page for as long as it remains online.

When you subscribe to a series, you have unlimited streaming access to all current videos on that series page, as well as any future videos that get added, as long as you maintain an active subscription.

I rented a title, but now I'd like to buy it. What can I do?

Contact us on JyoStar.com and we'll be happy to help you out.

Why am I getting an error message when I try to make a purchase on Vimeo On Demand?

If you’re seeing an error message that says “Oops. Something is not right here.” when trying to make a Vimeo On Demand purchase, this usually indicates an issue with your payment method. If you’ve made sure you’re entering your payment information correctly (or you’re seeing the message with multiple payment methods), please contact vimeo On Demand so they can dig in their end and provide more information.

How do I use a promo code?

If you’ve received a promo code, you can redeem it directly on a title’s Vimeo On Demand page. On the title’s page, click the button for the type of purchase your code is good for (Buy, Rent, or Subscribe). Then, in the purchase window that appears, click Apply promo code, enter the code, and click the Apply button. The purchase window will then update to reflect the discount.



If your code is for 100% off, you’ll need to click Continue to complete the transaction. If your code is for a partial discount, then you’ll need to enter valid payment information along with the code in order to complete the purchase.

I’m being told that my discount code is invalid. What’s going on?

If you’re certain that you’re entering the code correctly and you’re seeing a message that says “Sorry, that discount code appears to be invalid”, please contact us on jyoStar.com so we can investigate further (please include the code you’re trying to use in your email).

What happens when I pre-order a Film/TV Serial Title ?

When you pre-order a title on JyoStar App/ Vimeo On Demand page, you’re purchasing it before it has been released. You'll know that you are pre-ordering because the VOD page will display a banner that says, "Pre-order now."



When the title is released, your card will be charged and you’ll be notified via email from vimeo that the title is ready to view (you will also be notified if the release date changes or if the pre-order is canceled). Please note that if you pre-ordered a rental purchase, your rental period will begin as soon as the title is made available by JyoStar.

You must enter a valid credit card to pre-order on Vimeo On Demand Page.. You cannot pre-order with PayPal. If we’re unable to process your payment at the time of the title’s release, we’ll send you an email prompting you to try purchasing the title again. If you’re uncertain about the status of your pre-order purchase, contact vimeo so they can take a look on their end.

How do I know when would be the pre-ordered movie be ready to watch?

The JyoStar is responsible for releasing a title on its release date . As soon as a title has been made available for viewing on its release date, your card will be charged for your pre-order, and you’ll then have access to your purchase.You will also get email from vimeo as soon as the title is available for viewing!

How can I cancel a already purchased pre-order?

You can cancel a pre-order at any time by visiting your Purchases page, scrolling down to the Pre-orders section, and clicking the button that says Cancel.

I already bought this title! Why don’t I have access to it?

Sorry for the confusion! A few things may have happened with your purchase:

1. Did you choose to “rent” the title?
If so, please check the title’s rental period on its Vimeo On Demand page in case your rental period ended.

2. Is it possible that you purchased the film under a different Vimeo account?
In order to access your purchase, you must be logged in to the same Vimeo account you used to purchase the title originally. If you think you need help accessing the account you used to make your purchase, let us know and we can help out.

3. The charge may be pending.
If your billing statement doesn’t indicate that the charge went through successfully yet, it’s possible that the charge is still awaiting authorization. In this case, if the charge doesn’t go through successfully, it will eventually clear from your billing statement.

4. Your payment method may have failed.
If we aren’t able to charge your payment method successfully, then your purchase won’t go through, and you’ll need to try making the purchase again. If you have questions about the status of your purchase, contact us so we can take a look on our end.

What can I do if a video is pausing, stuttering, or choppy when I try to stream it?

Try turning off HD mode by clicking the "HD" icon at the bottom of the player (the HD icon will turn grey when HD is off). With HD turned off, the Vimeo On Demand player will dynamically serve the video at the best resolution your connection speed can handle at any given time. This should improve playback performance.

If this doesn’t help make playback smoother, make sure you have the latest version of Flash running on your computer.

If you’ve tried the above steps and you’re still having trouble, please test your Internet connection by going to this page and clickingTest Download Speed. Then, please contact vimeo (and include the test results from that page!) so they can further assist you.

I clicked the download button on a Vimeo On Demand page, but my download pauses or doesn’t finish. What can I do?

Try starting a fresh download (on the video’s VOD page) to see if that helps. If that doesn’t do the trick, make sure you’re using the most up-to-date version of your browser, or try downloading the video in another browser.

In addition, if you’re having trouble downloading a file over a wifi connection, try plugging an ethernet cable into your computer for a more consistent Internet connection.

I downloaded the video I purchased, but it doesn’t play on my computer. What can I do?

Try downloading the file again from the video’s Vimeo On Demand page, and make sure the download finishes completely. If that doesn’t do the trick, make sure you’re using the most up-to-date version of your browser, or try downloading the video in another browser.

How can I watch Vimeo On Demand videos with captions or subtitles?

While viewing your purchased video on a computer, click the CC button in the player's bottom toolbar. In the menu that appears, select the caption or subtitle track you’d like to see.



Please note: the CC button will only appear in the Vimeo player if captions or subtitles are available for the video you’re watching. The availability of captions or subtitles is dependent upon what the creator has chosen to provide.

If you’re viewing on Apple TV, you can find instructions for enabling captions here.

If I purchase a title for download, how do I download captions or subtitles to view with the video?

To watch a downloaded VOD title with captions or subtitles, you’ll need to download both the video and the caption files to your computer. You can download both of these files by going to the title’s VOD page, then clicking the Download button. In the window that appears, select the desired video file for download, then select the desired caption file for download. (You can also download these files by hovering over the title in your Libraryand clicking the green download icon.)

Once you’ve downloaded both files, you’ll need to use them with a media player that supports caption files.

We recommend VLC because it is compatible with a wide range of operating systems and file formats. You can download it for free here.
Once you've installed VLC, follow these steps:
  • Download a film/series from the title page or your Vimeo On Demand library.
  • Download the desired caption or subtitle file (should be .SRT format) from the download module.
  • Open the downloaded video file in VLC Media Player.
  • From the menu, click Subtitles, then Add Subtitle File...
  • Select the downloaded .SRT file, click Open, and enjoy!
